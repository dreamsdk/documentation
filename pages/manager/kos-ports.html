KallistiOS Ports

The KallistiOS Ports tab was made to manage all ports available to KallistiOS.

From that tab, you will have the possibility to install, update or uninstall a single port (by using the buttons located in the right panel) or install/uninstall all ports at once.

The installation status of the port is shown by a checkmark/black square before the port name, as you can see on the screenshot above.
