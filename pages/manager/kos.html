KallistiOS

The KallistiOS tab is very important, as it shows the installation status (installed or not installed), the version and the build date of your KallistiOS installation, plus a button to install/update the whole KallistiOS library and addons components.

To run an update or repair your KallistiOS installation, just click on the Update... button. This will start the update process, and the window will display the operations in real time.

Note: This process can take up some time, so please be patient.
